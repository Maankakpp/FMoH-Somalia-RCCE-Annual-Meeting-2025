Welcome & Core Objectives

Welcome to the Annual Review Meeting for Risk Communications and Community Engagement (RCCE). This meeting aims to review progress, share lessons learned, plan future strategies, and strengthen partnerships for effective RCCE across Somalia.

Core Objectives

Review Progress

Assess achievements against 2024 targets.

Share Learning

Discuss challenges and best practices.

Strengthen Partnerships

Align partner roles for a cohesive 2026 strategy.
